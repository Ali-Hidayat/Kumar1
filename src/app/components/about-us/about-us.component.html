Brand Promise

We build real estate properties that make the environment safe, secured, aspiring and beyond ordinary.

Founded by Mr. K.H. Oswal on 15 August 1966, the Kumar Properties Group is a well diversified, value driven enterprise. 135 projects completed and More than 35,000 satisfied and happy families are our brand ambassadors and our true strength.

Over 35,000 happy families from diverse socio-economic and cultural background, over 1,000 satisfied large, medium and small businesses, all enjoying the comforts and pleasures of living in environment-friendly, well-designed, fully-equipped residential complexes, or working out of swanky commercial premises.

That is the Kumar Properties’ record of achievement in core service offerings. A record of not mere construction, but creation of complete living, working and business spaces, with the latest amenities and facilities integrated in a holistic manner and backed by lifelong maintenance.

One of the largest players in Pune’s real estate market, which has constructed more than 32 million sq.ft., it is a brand that commands a premium over other real estate developers. Providing complete solutions to clients, the Group is a symbol of trust and integrity built through its superior quality work, timely project completion and personalized services.

Vision
To set new standards of excellence in the service of providing top quality living, working and business spaces. To maintain leadership through innovation in the field of design, technology and quality.

TAKING LEADERSHIP TO THE NEXT LEVEL

Steel in his veins

They say you are as strong as your foundation. A sterling example of this adage is Mr. Hitesh Jain, Director, Kumar Corp. Coming from a family that has a rich legacy of excellence in real estate, Mr. Jain, at a relatively young age of 42, was able to build on these inherent advantages, with steely determination and a flexible mind that is open to new, innovative ideas.

On the academic front, he holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering from MIT, Pune and this was further fortified with a Master’s in Business Administration degree from University of Cardiff, UK.
His experience abroad has given him new, exciting perspectives on the industry and the ever ardent student in him has been keen to bring those learnings into his work here.
At Kumar Corp, Mr. Jain oversees the engineering aspect of the construction, and he is instrumental in implementing a wide array of new age technologies that have gone a long way in the organisation being seen as a flag bearer of innovative technologies in the industry.
Today, Kumar Corp has gone from strength to strength, and even bravely weathered the storm which engulfed the entire industry not too long ago. This period was a huge learning experience for Mr. Jain and he has come out much stronger, and much more adept at blending ground realities with the oretical practices.
